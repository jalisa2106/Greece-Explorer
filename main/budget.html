💡 Tips to Save Money

  • Travel during shoulder seasons (April–June or September–October)
  • Eat at local tavernas and bakeries
  • Use public transport and walk where possible
  • Book accommodations and ferries in advance
  • Purchase combination tickets for archaeological sites
