Top Tourist Spots in Greece

ATHENS

The capital of Greece, rich in ancient ruins and vibrant city life.

CORFU

Known for its lush greenery, Venetian charm, and stunning beaches.

CRETE

Greece’s largest island with rugged mountains and ancient palaces.

DELPHI

Historic sanctuary once home to the famous Oracle of Apollo.

METEORA

Monasteries perched on towering rock pillars — surreal and sacred.

MYKONOS

Famous for its vibrant nightlife, whitewashed streets, and beaches.

NAFPLIO

A charming seaport town with fortresses, cafes, and cobbled lanes.

NAXOS

Less touristy, full of beaches, villages, and authentic Greek vibe.

RHODES

Home to medieval walls, sunny beaches, and ancient wonders.

SANTORINI

World-famous sunsets, cliff-side villages, and blue-domed churches.

ZAGORI

A mountain region with stone villages, bridges, and hiking trails.
